Featured Course

A Coastal Gem



Bandon Dunes Golf Course

Situated along the picturesque Oregon coastline, Bandon Dunes Golf Course stands as a testament to the natural beauty of the United States' golf landscape. Designed by architect David McLay Kidd and opened in 1999, this links-style course has earned its place among the premier golf destinations in the country.

Rankings:

  • Golf Digest (2022): #7 in "America's 100 Greatest Golf Courses."
  • Golf Digest (2022): #1 in "Oregon's Best Golf Courses."

Scorecard:

  • Par: 72.
  • Yardage: Approximately 6,732 yards (Championship tees).
  • Slope Rating: Varies based on tee selection.
  • Course Rating: Varies based on tee selection.

Description:

Bandon Dunes Golf Course is a masterpiece by architect David McLay Kidd, often regarded as a modern classic in golf course design. The layout emphasizes the raw beauty of the Oregon coastline, with stunning ocean views, natural dunes, and undulating terrain. The course is known for its traditional links-style features, including wide fairways, strategic bunkering, and challenging greens.

Key Features:

  • Ocean Views: Several holes provide panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, creating a visually stunning and immersive golf experience.
  • Natural Dunes: The course incorporates the natural dune landscape, offering a genuine links feel reminiscent of Scottish and Irish coastal courses.
  • Strategic Bunkering: Well-placed bunkers and hazards require thoughtful shot placement, adding a layer of strategy to each round.
  • Variety of Tees: Multiple tee options cater to golfers of all skill levels, providing an inclusive experience for both beginners and seasoned players.
  • Walking-Friendly: Bandon Dunes encourages walking, preserving the traditional spirit of the game. Caddies are available to enhance the experience.

Signature Holes:

  • Hole #4 ("Bastion"): A stunning par-4 that runs along the edge of the cliffs, offering breathtaking views of the ocean.
  • Hole #16 ("Toro"): A challenging par-4 with a green perched atop a dune, demanding accuracy and precision.

Bandon Dunes Golf Course exemplifies the spirit of links golf, providing a challenging yet enjoyable experience for golf enthusiasts. The combination of natural beauty, strategic design, and a commitment to the traditions of the game contributes to its esteemed reputation among the world's top golf destinations.
